The P=W Seminar is a seminar focusing on mathematics relating to the P=W conjecture. In particular on the Hitchin system side it will go over Ngo's support theorem.

Topic Segments for Talk(s):
  • Ngô 's Support Theorem (B.M.)
  • Non-abelian Hodge Theory (Marielle Ong)
  • Deligne Twistor Space (Jia-Choon Lee)
  • Topological Field Theory approach to cohomology of Character Varieties (B.M.)
  • Proof of the SL(2) P=W Conjecture (Sukjoo Lee)
  • Cluster structure following Gross--Hacking--Keel--Kontsevich (Sukjoo Lee)
